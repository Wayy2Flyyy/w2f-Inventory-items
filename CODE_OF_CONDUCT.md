# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and respectful community for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on technical work and documentation quality.
- Help keep the project useful for real FiveM communities.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Spam, low-effort noise, or misleading content
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.
